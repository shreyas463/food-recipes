Walnut brownie

Walnut Brownies are rich, chocolatey desserts, dense and fudgy, studded with crunchy walnuts for a delightful texture contrast.

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate - 200 grams
  • Butter - 150 grams
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Baking powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Eggs - 3
  • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
  • Chopped walnuts - 1 cup
  • Flour - 3/4 cup
  • Salt - a pinch
  • Cocoa powder - 1/4 cup

Steps

  1. Preparation: Melt chocolate and butter; mix in sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Add flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, salt, and walnuts.li>
  2. Baking: Pour into a greased pan, bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes, cool, and cut into squares.
